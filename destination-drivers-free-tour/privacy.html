When you enter the Destination Drivers Wine Tour Giveaway, we collect:

  • Email Address: Required for entry and winner notification

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Sweepstakes Administration: To process your entry and conduct the random drawing

  • Legal Compliance: To maintain records required by sweepstakes regulations
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent duplicate or fraudulent entries

3. Newsletter Communications

Newsletter subscription is completely optional.

If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter:

  • You will receive promotional emails about Destination Drivers tours, special offers, and wine country events
  • You may unsubscribe at any time using the "Unsubscribe" link in any newsletter email

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections

  • Access to entry data is limited to authorized personnel only

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

You have the right to:
